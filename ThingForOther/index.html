Jesko

The Jesko, a hypercar by Koenigsegg, features a 5.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine with up to 1,600 horsepower and a nine-speed Light Speed Transmission. Designed for extreme performance, it aims to surpass 300 mph, showcasing Koenigsegg's advanced engineering.

Ford Cortina

The 1972 Ford Cortina is a classic mid-sized sedan known for its reliable performance and popular appeal. Featuring a range of inline-four engines, it combined practicality with stylish design, making it a favorite in both family and racing circles.

Lexus IS F CCS-R

The Lexus IS F CCS-R is a track-focused variant of the IS F, featuring a 5.0-liter V8 engine with 416 horsepower. Enhanced with lightweight materials and racing modifications, it exemplifies Lexus's commitment to high-performance and motorsport excellence..
